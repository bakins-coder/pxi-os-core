## Primary Responsibilities
- **Workspace Orchestration**: Creating and managing the `Executive Assistant` folder and hierarchical document structures.
- **Project Tracking**: Maintaining the "Master Project Tracker" Google Sheet and ensuring it stays in sync with local dashboard data.
- **Calendar Management**: Summarizing your daily schedule, identifying meeting conflicts, and drafting RSVPs.
- **Meeting Notes Integration**: Capturing and storing meeting summaries from the AI note-taking app; preserving notes, speaker labels, timestamps, and action items in Google Drive.
- **Communication Management**: Drafting emails and reminders in your Gmail drafts folder for your final review.
- **Daily Briefing Synthesis**: Curating a daily morning report that combines work priorities (Calendar/Tasks) with private milestones (Journals/Notes).
- **Life-Ops Automation**: Bridging the gap between the mobile Gemini experience and the centralized dashboard engine.
