# Directive: MoneeWise Website Reconfiguration
**To**: Nova (Interface Visionary)
**From**: Prof (Orchestrator)

## Objective
Rebuild the MoneeWise website to align with the new strategic blueprint. The current site structure is fragmented and needs to be consolidated into a clear "Marketing Funnel" vs "PWA App" split.

## Visual & Character Strategy
- **Human Centricity**: Integrate high-quality, diverse, photo-realistic AI images of children and families. 
    - *Reference*: Look at **Ultra Education** for "Young Professionals" and **GoHenry** for "Everyday Relatability."
- **Avatar Integration**: Seamlessly composite 3D Ajapa avatars (Sage, Yanribo, Ajapsi) alongside humans. They should act as guides, pointing to CTAs and "bringing sections alive."
- **Organic Modernism**: Shift away from rigid grids. Use rounded corners, organic background blobs, and a soft, vibrant palette (Teals, Greens, Oranges).
- **High-Contrast CTAs**: Ensure buttons like "Join the Vault" use high-energy colors (Coral/Red/Orange) inspired by GoHenry.

## Key Blueprint Requirements

### 1. Navigation & Sitemap
- **Sticky Top Nav**: High-contrast "Join the Vault (Try for Free)" button.
- **Public Website Routes**:
    - `Home`: The Guided Journey (Hero Hook, 5-Jar, Age Toggles, Young CEOs, Analog-First, Family Vault, Pricing).
    - `How it Works`: 5-Jar System, Ajapa Shells, Parent Approvals.
    - `For Kids (5-12)`: Saving, gamified learning, AI Tortoise.
    - `For Teens (13-18)`: Bazaar, entrepreneurship, AI Business Mentor.
    - `Our Young CEOs`: AI-simulated case studies.
    - `Methodology`: Analog-First, Digital Wellness, 7 Laws.
    - `About Us`: Institutional Legacy (Nigerian Stock Exchange, CBN, SUBEB).
    - `Pricing`: Free vs Premium.
    - `Resources`: Podcasts, Blog, Word Ladders.

### 2. Homepage (The Hero Hook)
- **Visuals**: Use the prompt for the 10-year-old with the MoneeWise T-shirt and Tortoise Bank. (Note: Request assets from Prof if you need specific generated images).
- **Interactive**: Animated 5-Jar System graphic showing chore payment splitting.
- **Age Toggles**: Kids (5-12) vs Teens (13-18).
- **Young CEOs**: Carousel of simulated profiles (Sarah, David).
- **Analog-First Section**: Highlighting the "Digital Wellness Governor".
- **Family Vault Section**: Focusing on collaborative wealth building.

### 3. About Us Page
- Focus on the decade-long legacy.
- Feature the Nigerian Stock Exchange, CBN, and SUBEB history.
- Move trust markers here.

## Technical Notes
- Use `react` and `tailwind`.
- Ensure mobile responsiveness for the "Digital Wellness" theme.
- The PWA components should be clearly separated from the Public marketing pages.
